The app position is **"smartphones powering decentralized AI worldwide"**. The word "mining" does not appear anywhere in the listing or the UI — Google Play bans on-device cryptocurrency mining, and VylexAI does not do that.

### Full description (4000-char max)

```
VylexAI turns every smartphone into a node in a global, decentralized
AI compute network. Instead of renting from centralized data centers,
AI workloads are routed across millions of ordinary devices — yours
included.

◆ Two modes, one network

 • Provider. When you're charging on Wi-Fi with a safe battery
   temperature, your phone joins the network and runs lightweight AI
   tasks. You earn BSAI — the network's unit of account — for every
   verified contribution.

 • Client. Submit inference or lightweight fine-tuning tasks. They get
   dispatched across available devices and return to you quickly, at a
   fraction of cloud cost.

◆ What's under the hood

 • On-device inference with TensorFlow Lite and ONNX Runtime.
 • Task dispatch with N-way redundancy — results are only accepted
   when a majority of nodes agree.
 • Play Integrity attestation on every heartbeat so honest devices
   aren't crowded out by emulators.
 • Strict worker constraints: charging + Wi-Fi + battery-not-low +
   temperature ceiling. Your phone decides, not us.
 • Custodial wallet for the technical preview. Your BSAI balance is
   visible in-app from day one.

◆ Privacy, by design

 • Everything possible runs on device. Task inputs never pass through
   an intermediary.
 • Your account lives in the European Union. No transfers elsewhere.
 • Encrypted JWT via Android Keystore. No ads, no trackers, no
   advertising ID.
 • We require no location, contacts, SMS, or microphone permission.

◆ Made in Germany

VylexAI is a project of Dudaev Systems UG, Cottbus, Germany — aligned
with EU AI Act principles of transparency and auditability.

◆ Technical preview

This release is a technical preview. BSAI balances reflect network
contribution during the preview period and do not have monetary value
until the production economy launches separately.

Terms: https://vylexai.com/terms
Privacy: https://vylexai.com/privacy
Source: https://github.com/vylexAI/vylexAI-Android
```
